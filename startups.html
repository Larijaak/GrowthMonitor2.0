Startup-companies

This page includes data visualizations about the startup-companies located in the Tampere Region. The list of startup-companies is based on the startup survey conducted by Business Tampere in the autumn of 2020. This company list is enriched by data from various sources.

On this page you can view information about the founding years, locations, industries as well as the revenue and employment count of the startup-companies. You can also view the list of the companies.
